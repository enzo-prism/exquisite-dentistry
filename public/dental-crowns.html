Porcelain Dental Crowns

Protect and perfect compromised teeth with crowns that mirror natural enamel down to the translucency.

Dr. Aguil designs crowns that reinforce weakened teeth and keep your smile photo-ready. Whether you’ve had a root canal, a fracture, or an aging restoration, we’ll rebuild strength without sacrificing beauty.

  • Digital Design: 3D scans capture every contour to guide precise preparations.
  • Lab Partnership: Top-tier ceramists craft crowns using layered porcelain for lifelike results.
  • Bite Harmony: We fine-tune occlusion so your jaw, TMJ, and smile stay comfortable.

Benefits

  • Strength & Longevity: Crowns shield fragile teeth from fractures and temperature sensitivity.
  • Cosmetic Upgrade: Customize shade and shape to enhance symmetry and confidence.
  • Predictable Process: Two visits with luxurious temporaries in between keep you comfortable.

Treatment Timeline

  1. 01. Consultation & Scan — Digital impressions capture your current smile and bite dynamics.
  2. 02. Preparation & Temporary — We shape the tooth, place a custom temporary, and send specs to the lab.
  3. 03. Final Bonding — Your porcelain crown is bonded in place and polished for perfection.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do crowns look natural?
Yes. We control translucency, texture, and shade so they blend seamlessly with surrounding teeth.

How do I care for my crown?
Brush, floss, and schedule cleanings every six months. A nightguard protects against clenching or grinding.

Are same-day crowns available?
For select cases we can use chairside milling, but lab-fabricated porcelain delivers the most precise, cosmetic outcome.
