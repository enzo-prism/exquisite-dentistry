Natural-Looking Dental Bridges

When implants aren’t the right fit, a custom porcelain bridge closes the gap with strength, symmetry, and confidence.

A dental bridge replaces one or more missing teeth by anchoring porcelain crowns to neighboring teeth. We design each bridge with ceramists who understand Los Angeles aesthetics, so your smile looks seamless on camera and in real life.

  • Digital Planning: We scan your teeth with iTero so we can map bite forces, gumline contour, and occlusion for long-term comfort.
  • Artisan Fabrication: Our lab partners craft layered porcelain that perfectly matches translucency, shade, and surface texture.
  • Comprehensive Protection: We review nightguard needs, hygiene strategies, and maintenance visits to keep your bridge pristine.

Benefits

  • Immediate Transformation: Restore chewing, speech, and symmetry in just two visits.
  • Conservative Approach: Ideal when implants aren’t possible or desired due to bone, timelines, or medical considerations.
  • Cosmetic Precision: The final bridge is indistinguishable from your natural enamel.

Treatment Timeline

  1. 01. Smile Design Session — Digital scans, photography, and shade mapping guide your blueprint.
  2. 02. Preparation & Temporaries — Supporting teeth are shaped and protected with luxurious temporaries.
  3. 03. Final Placement — We bond the bridge, refine your bite, and review maintenance.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long do dental bridges last?
With proper hygiene and regular checkups, bridges often last 10–15 years or more. We’ll share specific tips for flossing and care to extend longevity.

Are implants better?
Implants are a fantastic option, but bridges remain ideal when bone grafting isn’t preferred, multiple implants would raise costs, or a faster solution is needed.

Will insurance help?
Most PPO plans contribute toward bridges. Our concierge team verifies benefits before treatment so you know your exact investment.
