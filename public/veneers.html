What Are Porcelain Veneers?

Porcelain veneers are ultra-thin shells of medical-grade ceramic that are custom-crafted to fit over the front surface of your teeth. They provide an immediate transformation for teeth that are discolored, chipped, gapped, or slightly misaligned.

Benefits of Porcelain Veneers

  • Correct Multiple Issues: Fix chips, gaps, stains, and minor misalignment in one treatment
  • Natural-Looking Results: Custom color and translucency matching for seamless integration
  • Long-Lasting Durability: High-quality porcelain can last 10-15 years with proper care
  • Minimal Tooth Preparation: Conservative treatment preserves most of your natural tooth structure
  • Stain Resistance: Porcelain resists coffee, tea, and wine stains better than natural teeth
  • Custom Design: Tailored to your facial features and desired aesthetic goals

The Veneer Process

  1. Consultation: Comprehensive examination and smile design planning
  2. Preparation: Minimal tooth preparation to accommodate the veneers
  3. Impressions: Precise molds taken for custom veneer fabrication
  4. Temporary Veneers: Temporary restorations while your custom veneers are crafted
  5. Final Placement: Bonding of your custom porcelain veneers
  6. Follow-up: Care instructions and follow-up appointments
