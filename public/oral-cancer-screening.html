VELscope® Oral Cancer Screenings

Protect your long-term health with fast, non-invasive screenings built into every hygiene visit.

Oral cancer is most treatable when caught early. We use VELscope® technology to highlight abnormal tissue long before it’s visible to the eye.

Whether you’re a routine patient or new to LA, we can add a screening to any appointment for added peace of mind.

  • Blue-Light Technology: VELscope’s fluorescence visualization reveals cellular changes under the surface.
  • Baseline Photography: We document findings so we can compare visits and spot subtle developments.
  • Specialist Network: If we see anything suspicious, we handle referrals and follow-up coordination immediately.

Benefits

  • Peace of Mind: Know that proactive screenings are part of every visit, not an optional add-on.
  • Holistic Wellness: We look at lifestyle, medications, and immune factors that influence risk.
  • Fast & Comfortable: No dyes, rinses, or discomfort—just a quick scan during your exam.

Treatment Timeline

  1. 01. Health Review — We discuss risk factors like tobacco use, HPV, and family history.
  2. 02. Visual & Palpation Exam — Your clinician checks cheeks, tongue, lymph nodes, and soft tissue.
  3. 03. VELscope Scan — Blue light highlights areas that may need closer monitoring or a specialist referral.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who should get screened?
Everyone. Risk increases with age, alcohol, and tobacco, but we see cases across all lifestyles. That’s why it’s part of every cleaning visit here.

Does insurance cover oral cancer screenings?
Most PPO plans consider it part of a comprehensive exam. If there’s any out-of-pocket cost, we’ll let you know beforehand.

What happens if you spot something?
We’ll photograph the area, monitor it closely, and coordinate with trusted specialists for biopsy or further evaluation if needed.
