## Dental Veneers

Veneers come in all ranges of quality. At Exquisite Dentistry, our veneers are made of the highest-quality porcelain that are thin yet durable. The veneers are applied to teeth using a strong bonding material after your natural teeth are trimmed down to accommodate them. Once secure, veneers instantly remedy cracks, stains, or gaps while also aligning teeth into an ideal position. However, with more challenging cases, the process can be done in as few as three to four visits. It may take a couple more to ensure the optimum fit, shape, and color. The advantage of veneers is that you can refine them until you achieve the perfect result for your smile. Once you’re done, you have a beautiful smile that will last for years and years with proper care and regular visits to the dentist for oral hygiene check-ups and exams.

### Dental Bonding

Bonding begins with the application of a flexible dental resin onto the teeth. Once in place, the resin is hardened with a special light that binds it to the tooth. While bonding offers several benefits, such as cavity filling, reshaping teeth, or closing gaps, it’s worth noting that the hardened resin isn’t as durable as the porcelain used in veneers and that the bonding material doesn’t lend itself as well to fine micro-adjustments in shape and color.

## The Limitations of Bonding

While bonding is versatile and reliable, it only offers some of the capabilities of veneers. The dental resin used in bonding is not as strong or durable as porcelain, making it prone to chipping, and its porous nature makes it susceptible to stains and fading. Therefore, bonding is primarily recommended for filling cavities and making minor adjustments to teeth.

## The Benefits of Veneers

Thanks to the customizable nature of veneers, patients can enjoy a tailored solution that precisely matches the color, shape, and size of their natural teeth, resulting in a beautiful and seamless enhancement of their smile. Veneers offer a long-lasting and durable option for improving the appearance of teeth, providing not only aesthetic benefits but also functional advantages for years to come. While veneers provide a long-lasting solution for achieving a radiant smile, maintaining good oral habits remains crucial. It’s essential to be mindful of dietary choices, to use a mouthguard at night and during sports activities for added protection, and to adhere to a consistent regimen of brushing and flossing daily. These practices help preserve the integrity of your veneers and contribute to overall oral health and longevity.
