Title: 5 Ways to Improve Oral Care While You’re at Work

Busy schedules can make it tough to give your teeth attention during the workday. A few small habits can keep your mouth feeling fresh, even when meetings and deadlines stack up. Here are five easy ways to care for your smile while you are on the job.

1) Pick a predictable time. Choose a moment that already fits into your daily rhythm—after lunch, after your afternoon latte, or before you head home. Set a phone reminder so you do not forget. Once it becomes part of your routine, that quick cleaning feels effortless.

2) Build a compact care kit. Keep a travel toothbrush, small toothpaste, floss or floss picks, and sugar-free gum in a zipper pouch or desk drawer. When everything is ready to go, you can freshen up quickly and discreetly wherever you work.

3) Rinse when you cannot brush. If the sink is busy or you are in the car between appointments, swish water around your mouth for thirty seconds. This simple step clears away food particles and lessens stains until you can brush properly.

4) Be mindful of shared spaces. Use the restroom rather than the break room sink, toss used floss into the trash, and wipe the counter when you finish. A little courtesy keeps coworkers comfortable and makes it easier to stick with your new habit.

5) Stay consistent. The more you repeat these steps, the more natural they feel. Give yourself credit for protecting your health and confidence, even on hectic days.
